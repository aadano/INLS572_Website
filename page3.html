Student Life at UNC Chapel Hill

At Carolina, student life extends far beyond the classroom. With over 800 student organizations, vibrant on-campus housing, and a tight-knit community rooted in tradition, UNC offers an experience that shapes students for life.

Explore Student Life

Student Organizations

UNC Chapel Hill is home to more than 800 registered student organizations spanning academics, arts, culture, service, and recreation. Whether you want to lead a club sport, join an a cappella group, or participate in student government, there is a place for every Tar Heel.

  • Greek Life: 40+ fraternities and sororities
  • Club Sports: 40+ competitive club teams
  • Cultural Organizations: 100+ groups celebrating diverse backgrounds
  • Community Service: 150+ volunteer and service-learning groups

Housing & Dining

On-campus housing at UNC provides first-year students with a welcoming home close to classes, libraries, and campus events. Upperclassmen can choose from residence halls, apartment-style living, or off-campus neighborhoods.

  • 14 residence halls housing approximately 4,400 students
  • Living-Learning Communities for students with shared interests
  • 15+ dining locations including The Pit, the hub of campus dining
  • Meal plan options flexible enough to fit every schedule
